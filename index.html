Empowering Everyone

From multinational corporations to rural communities, AetherSim is changing how the world builds and innovates.

Enterprise

"How did Company B sales double in the last month?"

Because they made the engineering models of their batteries available in the app. Potential customers did early evaluation by making digital prototypes in-app.

Many liked what they saw; some placed orders while others contacted Company B directly.

The IP Safety

There is no risk. Companies have full control over public model aspects. All simulations stay in the cloud.

Research

"How did Lab B get so much funding recently?"

They made an approximate engineering model, based on research, available in AetherSim. Companies used it for early evaluation of research utility.

The positive impression led companies to finance the research to speed up time-to-market.

Social Benefit

It reduces the time for research to reach the market and benefit humanity. It's a win-win-win!

Community

"How did the neighbouring village develop so much?"

They simulated power potential for commercially available wind mills. The community presented these results to officials and NGOs.

The project became a reality, initiated not by engineers, but by a grade 10 school student.

Simplicity

The UI is so intuitive, anyone can assemble and simulate. AI is there to help figure things out!
